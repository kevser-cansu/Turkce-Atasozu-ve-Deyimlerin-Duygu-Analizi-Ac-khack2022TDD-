scale score
sum of positive and negative scores(negative sentiment from -1 to -5 and positive score from 1 to 5)
//////////////////////////////
dual score
a positive(from 1 to 5) and a negative score(from -5 to -1)
////////////////////
trinary score
a positive(from 1 to 5), negative score(from -5 to -1) and a neutral score(-1,0,1)
////////////////////////7
binary score
a binary result(1 is positive; -1 is negative)
